Privacy Policy

This privacy policy ("Privacy Policy") and any other documents referred to herein set forth the basis on which any data we collect from you, or that you provide to us, in connection with the software application SimpleWod ("Application") will be processed by us. Please read the following carefully to understand our practices regarding your information and how we will collect and use your information.

Automatically Collected Information

The Application may collect certain information automatically, including, but not limited to, the type of mobile device you use, your mobile unique device ID, the IP address of your mobile device, your mobile operating system, the type of mobile Internet browsers you use, and information about the way you use the Application.

Advertising and Location

The Application provides advertisements using the AdMob library by Google. Interest-based advertisements may be shown based on your Identifier for Advertisers (IDFA), and Google may use this identifier to serve ads to users based on their use of the Application and advertisements served to other apps on the mobile device. The Application may also collect broad, non-specific geographic location to help serve advertisements. More information regarding Google's Privacy Policy can be found here: https://www.google.com/intl/en/policies/privacy/ . More information on "How Google uses data when you use our partners' sites or apps" can be found here: https://www.google.com/policies/privacy/partners/.

If you’d like to opt-out from third party use of this type of information to help serve targeted advertising, please visit the section entitled “Opt-out” below.

Mobile Analytics

The Application collects anonymous user statistics using the Firebase Analytics Library by Google. The data collected cannot be used to identify individual users and is used to gather statistics on how the Application is used in order to obtain feedback on how to improve the Application. The Application may collect information such as how often you use the Application, the events that occur within the Application, aggregated usage, performance data, and the mobile application marketplace or network used to download the Application. See the Firebase Analytics terms for more information: https://firebase.google.com/terms/analytics.

If you’d like to opt-out from ours and any third party use of this type of information to help improve the app, please visit the section entitled “Opt-out” below.

Opt-out

There are multiple opt-out options for users of this Application:

You may, at any time, stop all collection of information by the Application by uninstalling the Application. You may use the standard uninstall processes as may be available as part of your mobile device or via the mobile application marketplace or network.

You may, at any time, opt-out from the use of information to serve targeted advertising by advertisers and/or third party network advertisers by disabling your Identifier for Advertisers (IDFA) in your mobile device settings.

We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect changes to our information practices. You are advised to consult this Privacy Policy regularly for any changes, as continued use is deemed approval of all changes.

Your Consent

By using the Application, you are consenting to our processing of your information as set forth in this Privacy Policy now and as amended by us.
